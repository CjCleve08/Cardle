How to Play Cardle

üéØ Objective

Guess the 5-letter word before your opponent! Use strategy cards to gain an advantage.

üìù How to Play

  1. Choose a Card: At the start of your turn, select a power card from your hand
  2. Make a Guess: Enter a 5-letter word and submit
  3. Get Feedback: Letters turn green (correct position), yellow (in word, wrong position), or gray (not in word)
  4. Use Clues: Use the feedback to narrow down the word
  5. Win: First player to guess the word correctly wins!

üÉè Power Cards

Each turn, you can play a card to help yourself or hinder your opponent:

  • Help Cards: Give you advantages like extra turns, hidden guesses, or hidden feedback
  • Hurt Cards: Disrupt your opponent with false feedback, card locks, or hidden guesses
  • Special Cards: Some cards can be chained together for powerful combos

üéÆ Turn Structure

  1. Wait for your turn (indicated at the top of the screen)
  2. Select a card from your hand
  3. Type your 5-letter guess
  4. Press Enter or click Submit
  5. Watch the feedback and plan your next move

üí° Tips

  • Start with common letters (E, A, R, T, O) to get information quickly
  • Use cards strategically - save powerful cards for key moments
  • Pay attention to your opponent's guesses to learn about the word
  • Build your deck in the Deck Builder to customize your strategy
  • Use the chat to communicate with your opponent (or distract them!)

  • Keyboard: Type letters directly or use the on-screen keyboard
  • Enter: Submit your guess
  • Backspace: Delete letters
  • Chat: Click the chat icon to toggle the chat panel
